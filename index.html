I am currently working at Systal Technology Solutions as a Level 1 SOC Analyst.

At Systal Technology Solutions, I work in our in-house Security Operations Centre (SOC) where my contributions in information security analysis and defensive security help the organisation and others protect their online environments.

My certifications, including those from IBM, Cloudflare, and Cisco bolster my analytical skills, equipping us to preemptively counteract digital threats. I have also entered into the world of Digital Forensics where I have learned about the vast subject of Android forensics and how to obtain information from an Android device to aid in an investigation.

In the near future, I aim to achieve some more certifications from Microsoft (the SC-200) and from IBM (the IBM certified analyst - security QRadar SIEM), along with progressing my studies at Napier.
